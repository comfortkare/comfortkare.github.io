About Us

Comfort Kare is committed to providing premium tissue products that combine softness, strength, and reliability. From home care to personal hygiene, we bring comfort to every touchpoint in your life.

Established as a Partnership firm in the year 2017, we “Comfort Kare” are a leading manufacturer of a wide range of Facial Tissues and Wet Wipes. Based in Tamil Nadu, India, we have built a strong foundation with an efficient infrastructure unit that plays a vital role in our business growth.
